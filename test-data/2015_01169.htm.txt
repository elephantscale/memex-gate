An appeal having been taken to this Court by the above-named appellant from a
judgment of the Supreme Court, New York County (Charles H. Solomon, J.), rendered
on or about October 2, 2012, said appeal having been argued by counsel for the
respective parties, due deliberation having been had thereon, and finding the sentence not
excessive,

it is unanimously ordered that the judgment so appealed from be and the same is
hereby affirmed. Concur—Tom, J.P., Friedman, Andrias, DeGrasse and Gische,
JJ.
