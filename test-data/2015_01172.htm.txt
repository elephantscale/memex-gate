An appeal having been taken to this Court by the above-named appellant from a
judgment of the Supreme Court, Bronx County (John W. Carter, J.), rendered on or about
January 31, 2012, said appeal having been argued by counsel for the respective parties,
due deliberation having been had thereon, and finding the sentence not excessive, it is
unanimously ordered that the judgment so appealed from be and the same is hereby
affirmed. Concur—Tom, J.P., Friedman, Andrias, DeGrasse and Gische, JJ.
